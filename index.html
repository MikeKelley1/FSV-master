ABOUT FLY SUN VALLEY

Fly Sun Valley is the only fully insured and pemitted paragliding operation of the Sun Valley Resort, conducting flights in cooperation with the Sun Valley Company and through an exclusive commercial permit issued by the US Forest Service.
